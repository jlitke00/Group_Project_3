FAQS

Have questions? We have answers!

What is bartering?

Bartering is the exchange of goods or services between users
ithout using money. Trade what you have for what you need!

What if I change my mind?

Before confirming a trade, you can cancel or adjust your offer.
Once the trade is accepted, you can't undo it.

How do I know if a trade is fair?

Fairness is up to both parties. We recommend clear communication
and understanding the value of items/services being exchanged.
